## Identity & access how‑tos

This section will contain guides for configuring and operating single sign‑on and access control, such as:

- Connecting to external identity providers (IdPs).
- Managing groups and group‑based access.
- Customizing Keycloak behavior and sessions.
- Protecting apps with Authservice.
- Handling service accounts and device flows.
