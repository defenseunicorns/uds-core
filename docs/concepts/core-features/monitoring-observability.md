## Monitoring & observability in UDS Core

Monitoring and observability in UDS Core provide visibility into the health and performance of the platform and its
workloads.

This page will outline:

- How metrics are collected and stored.
- What dashboards and alerts are typically available.
- How platform and application owners should think about instrumenting their services.
