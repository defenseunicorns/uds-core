UDS Core enforces secure workload behavior through [Pepr](https://docs.pepr.dev/) admission policies. Every resource submitted to the cluster passes through Pepr before being persisted, where mutations auto-correct common misconfigurations and validations block non-compliant resources.

These guides help you resolve policy violations, create exemptions when needed, and audit your cluster's security posture. For background on how policies and exemptions work, see the [Policy & Compliance concepts](/concepts/core-features/policy-and-compliance/).
